IT Information

Foróige Email accounts and Staff Portal

Over the coming days you will receive an email that will have the details of your new foroige.ie email account- this username and password also grants you access to our intranet, the Staff Portal. (The staff portal is our primary location for all policies, procedures and news about the organisation- make sure to visit it at least once a day, and use the search to find the answers to questions you may have).

Once you have the Staff Portal open, you'll see shortcuts in the middle of the main page to your email, calendars and docs- we will be providing training in docs at a later stage, but you can start using your email and calendar immediately.

You may access your email and the staff portal from any computer with an Internet connection using these login details; just remember the staff.foroige.ie address!


Google Chrome

Download and install Google Chrome: it's the fastest and most secure Internet browser, and the best way to access your email, the staff portal and the Internet generally.


Agresso

Agresso is the business support software used by Foróige. It records personnel information and accounting records of the organisation. Within Agresso you will be required to raise purchase requisitions, work diaries and expense claims, your direct line supervisor will provide you with all the necessary training.

New Computers

If you are to receive a new computer, we will contact you and let you know when it is ready to collect. It will be pre-installed with all the software you will need, including Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint, etc.) and antivirus and encryption software (encryption is only needed on laptops).


Any further questions or in need of assistance contact us by email: foroige@support.ie
